The Heart of Sullia

While families tend to the land, we tend to their children.

Many of our families are the backbone of Sullia's plantations, working as daily wage earners. While they tend to the land, we tend to their children. We provide a dignified space where financial hardship or physical disability never stands in the way of a child's right to a quality education and a joyful childhood.
