The founder's message

A Labor of Love in the Western Ghats.

“In the quiet hills of Sullia, among the mist and the rubber trees, we saw a need that couldn't be ignored. For over two decades, we have opened our hearts to children who the world often overlooks. At M.B. Foundation, we aren't just teachers, we are family. Every child who walks through the doors of Sandeep Special School is a testament to the fact that with enough care, every life can bloom.”

— Mr. & Mrs. Sadashiv

Why this matters

Therapy, schooling, and care should be local and accessible.

Local context

Children of rubber plantation workers often have no access to specialized therapy.

The school responds to a practical gap in Sullia, where many families need support but cannot easily travel for consistent specialized care.

Child-friendly care

Dignified rooms, patient teaching, and a setting designed for growth.

Every child deserves a safe environment that respects their pace, builds confidence, and helps them move toward fuller participation in society.
